Imagery Guidelines
/* Clarify Imagery Rules */

Photos & illustrations:
✓  Real founders, candid and warm — natural light
✓  Rounded-square crop on portraits: border-radius 16px
✓  Soft gradient backgrounds (sky, peach, mint)
✓  Warm backdrop for testimonials
✗  Stock boardroom photography (suits, handshakes)
✗  Dark / dramatic photography — keep it soft and airy
✗  Hard shadows — use soft ambient only

✓  Real Clarify UI with realistic-looking data
✓  Rounded frames: border-radius 12–16px

Brand Strategy

Core Belief

CRM should work for you. Not the other way around.

Founders didn't start companies to spend their days logging calls and updating fields. Clarify exists to make that burden disappear — so founders can focus on relationships and revenue.

Mission
Mission Statement
To give every founder the unfair advantage of a world-class sales operation — without the overhead of one.
Who We Serve
01 — Founders

Founder-led sales

Rep handles briefings, follow-ups, field updates. They just show up to sell.

02 — Early Sales Teams

Small GTM teams

Five reps can operate like fifteen with automated pipeline and AI-enriched contacts.

03 — Technical Founders

Builders who hate busywork

Flexible data model, open API, zero-setup AI — it just works out of the box.

04 — Community

The startup ecosystem

Our customers are our beta testers, advocates, and loudest roadmap contributors.

Brand Attributes
⚡
Effortless

Every feature reduces your work. Default-to-automatic is the rule, not the exception.

🎯
Focused

We serve founders and early GTM teams. We're the best at your stage — not everything to everyone.

🤝
Warm & Human

CRM is about relationships. Our brand feels like it was built by founders, for founders.

🧠
Intelligent

AI isn't bolted on — it's the core. Rep, enrichment, and pipeline AI are all native.

✨
Joyful

Using Clarify should feel delightful. Clean UI, smooth interactions. The CRM shouldn't feel like a chore.

🚀
Ambitious

We're building toward a CRM that acts like a top-performing sales rep. The best is ahead.

Brand Persona
Our Persona
Think of Clarify as that founder friend who has every system dialed in — but never makes you feel bad about yours. Direct without being cold. Competent without being smug. They show up prepared, leave things better than they found them, and genuinely want you to win.

When Clarify talks to you, it sounds like someone who's been in the trenches — not a SaaS brand trying to seem relatable.

Copy, Voice & Tone

What is Clarify? — By Length
Long
The way founders sell hasn't changed in decades. You jump between tools, manually log calls, update fields you'll never look at again, and still miss follow-ups. The CRM was supposed to help — but it became another job.

Clarify is the autonomous CRM built for the way founders actually work. It joins your calls, reads your email, enriches your contacts, and keeps your pipeline current — without you asking it to. Rep, our AI sales agent, briefs you before every meeting, drafts follow-ups, and answers questions about any deal, anytime.
Medium
Clarify is the autonomous CRM for founder-led startups — one that stays in sync with your email and calendar, updates your pipeline automatically, and runs Rep, your personal AI sales agent, to brief every meeting and handle every follow-up.
Short
Clarify is the autonomous CRM that updates itself, manages your pipeline, and helps you close more deals — so you can stop CRMing and start selling.
Voice Principles
✓ Direct
✓ Lead with the benefit: "Less CRMing. More selling."
✓ Back claims with numbers: "90% of data entry, handled."
✗ Don't bury the lede. First line must earn the second.
✓ Warm
✓ Show empathy: "You didn't start a company to log calls."
✓ Celebrate customer wins like your own — they are.
✗ Don't manufacture relatability. Be genuine.
✓ Confident
✓ Let customers speak: "I'm not moving off of it."
✓ Make bold claims you can back: "The prep you'd do if you had time."
✗ Don't hedge everything. Confidence is a feature.
✓ Playful (when earned)
✓ Humor lands when it fits: "The CRM that configures itself."
✗ No jokes in error states, billing, or stressful moments.
✗ Wit must land instantly — no humor that needs context.
Tropes to Avoid
Em dash as drama
✗ Clarify is more than a CRM — it's a movement.
✓ Clarify is more than a CRM. It's how great founders sell.
"Leverage" as default
✗ Leverage AI to accelerate your pipeline velocity.
✓ Use AI to close deals faster.
"In today's world"
✗ In today's fast-paced sales environment...
✓ Sales moves fast. Your CRM should keep up.
Hollow affirmations
✗ "Great question!" "Absolutely!" "Certainly!"
✓ Just answer. No compliment needed.

Copy Rules
/* Clarify Copy Rules */

✓  Sentence case: "Start for free" not "Start For Free"
✓  Oxford comma: "calls, emails, and pipeline"
✓  En dash for ranges: "3–5 minutes"
✓  Product names capitalized: Clarify, Rep, Pipeline AI

✓  CTAs: short imperatives, sentence case
   "Start for free"  "Get to know Rep →"  "See it live →"
   ✗ Not: "Click here to learn more about our platform"

✗  Never: "robust", "synergize", "leverage", "comprehensive"
✗  Never start with: "We are excited to announce..."
✗  No hollow affirmations
